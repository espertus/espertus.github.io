Eliminating bias

Participants made these suggestions regarding various kinds of bias: ``Examine policies and procedures for possible bias in reviewing, hiring, and promotion.'' ``Clarify and publicize policies requiring nonsexist language usage in all published material.'' ``Give your junior people access and exposure to the funding process.''
