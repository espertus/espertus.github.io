Be good at what you do

Many gave this advice: ``The most important thing is to believe in what you're doing, do a good job, and don't be shy about talking about it and advertising it.''

``Never think in terms of gender where work and ability are concerned.''

One person put it succinctly: ``Be good, hungry, driven, and aggressive.''
