Introduction

I got my Bachelor's and Master's degree and am working on my PhD in computer science at MIT. After seven-and-a-half years at MIT, I still love the place, but I've also seen serious problems. I'm going to discuss three aspects of my MIT experience: my computer science education, my development as a feminist, and other women's experiences at MIT.
