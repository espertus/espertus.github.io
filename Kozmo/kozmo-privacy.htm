Privacy Policy
 
A customer's right to personal privacy is of paramount importance to Kozmo. When you order a product, Kozmo will ask you to set up an account asking for your name, email address, mailing address, home phone number, credit card number and other information. This information is collected to make ordering as easy and convenient as possible for the customer. You also have the opportunity to fill out a brief demographic questionnaire to allow Kozmo to better serve you. This survey is strictly optional and is not in any way necessary to become a Kozmo member.

All personal information is stored to make future orders as easy and convenient as possible for the customer. It will not be shared with a third party.

Kozmo protects account information against unauthorized access or release. Kozmo will not give, sell, rent or loan any identifiable personal information to any third party, unless legally required to do so. Kozmo may share non-personal, summary, or aggregate customer data with partners and other third parties.

Security
 
Kozmo backs every credit card purchase or rental by secure service software, which encrypts information using SSL technology, ensuring that Internet transactions stay private and protected.

Credit Card Safety
 
If any unauthorized use of your credit card occurs from a Kozmo purchase, notify your credit card company in accordance with its reporting rules and procedures. If your credit card company finds credit card fraud, but does not waive your entire liability for unauthorized charges, Kozmo will reimburse you for the remaining liability, up to a maximum of fifty U.S. dollars ($50.00). This guarantee only applies to credit card purchases made using Kozmo's secure server.

Consent and Changes to Privacy Policy
 
By signing up as a Kozmo member, you consent to collection and use of information by Kozmo. Any policy changes will only affect information collected after the policy change. Information will always be guarded according to the standards in effect at the time the information was collected.

Your Comments
 
Kozmo welcomes our customer's feedback. If there are any questions about privacy policies, please e-mail privacy@kozmo.com
